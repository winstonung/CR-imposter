Imposter - Clash Royale Edition

Find who doesn't have the same card as everyone else.

You can only describe your card with one word at a time, and you CANNOT use any of the card details in your words.

For example with Giant, you cannot say the following:

  • "Giant"
  • "Rare"
  • "Troop"
  • "Melee"
  • "5 elixir"
  • "2016"
